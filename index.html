University of Florida's Century Tower

By Micaela Leiva

According to this Wikipedia page, the Century Tower is a 48 meter tall building located in the heart of the University of Florida's campus in Gainesville, Florida.

Click the image below to see the entrance to the tower!

The door of Century Tower by Ebyabe, CC BY-SA 3.0 http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/, via Wikimedia Commons.

According to UF's college of Planning, Design & Construction, the tower was inaugurated in 1953 which celebrated a centennial since the university was founded in 1853. Also, the Century Tower was dedicated to the University of Florida's alumni that lost their lives during World War I and World War II.

A plaque stands in front of the tower to conmemorate both past and present students at the University of Florida. Take a look at the plaque below:

The plaque of Century Tower by Ebyabe, CC BY-SA 3.0 http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/, via Wikimedia Commons.

The Century Tower at the University of Florida is part of the University of Florida's history and should be remembered as an icon for unity and rememberance.
